About Me

Hi! My name is Edwin, and since September 1, 2024, I've been studying to become a full-stack developer at Chas Academy. I first became interested in programming around 2017, but unfortunately, I didn't pursue it until now. It's been a lot of fun, but also challenging, to learn all the new concepts that come with programming, and I'm excited to keep improving.

I'm a very social person who loves meeting new people and learning new things. Sometimes, this can get in my way because I tend to say yes to more things than I can handle. However, it has also been beneficial, as I've had the opportunity to learn a lot of new skills and meet many inspiring people along the way.

Right now, I spend three days a week in Arboga, my hometown, because of my part-time job as a salesperson at a company called Telways. The other four days, I stay in Stockholm to attend school. It has been a bit of a challenge to balance everything, but so far, it's been going well. However, i'm now looking for a job in Stockholm so I can stay there full-time.
